Major Achievements

Nobel Prize in Physics

Awarded in 1921 for his services to Theoretical Physics and discovery of the photoelectric effect.

Theory of Relativity

Developed the special and general theories of relativity, revolutionizing our understanding of space, time, and gravity.

Mass-Energy Equivalence

Formulated the world-famous equation E = mc², showing the relationship between mass and energy.
